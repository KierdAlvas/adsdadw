Rimac Revera

The Rimac C_Two, later rebranded as the Rimac Nevera, is an all-electric hypercar developed by the Croatian automotive company Rimac Automobili. The Nevera is named after a Mediterranean storm that symbolizes its incredible speed, power, and capability. It is one of the fastest and most technologically advanced electric hypercars in the world, boasting unparalleled performance, cutting-edge technology, and luxurious craftsmanship. Here’s a comprehensive look at the Rimac Nevera:

1. Design and Exterior

Body Style:

The Rimac Nevera is a two-door, two-seat electric hypercar with a sleek, aerodynamic profile designed for extreme performance and efficiency.

Aerodynamics:

The Nevera features active aerodynamics, including an adaptive front hood, adjustable rear diffuser, and rear wing, all of which adjust automatically based on driving conditions to reduce drag or enhance downforce as needed. The design helps provide stability at high speeds while maximizing efficiency.

Material:

The Nevera’s body and monocoque chassis are made of carbon fiber, ensuring lightweight construction while maintaining exceptional strength and rigidity. Rimac claims it to be the largest single-piece carbon-fiber monocoque ever made in the automotive industry.

Lighting:

The car is equipped with advanced LED lighting, giving it a modern, futuristic look that aligns with its high-tech identity.

2. Powertrain and Performance

Electric Motors:

The Rimac Nevera is powered by four electric motors, one for each wheel. This setup enables precise torque vectoring and independent power distribution to each wheel.

Total Output:

The combined output of the motors is an astonishing 1,914 horsepower (1,408 kW) and 1,741 lb-ft (2,360 Nm) of torque, making it one of the most powerful production cars ever.

Transmission:

The Nevera has no traditional gearbox; each motor drives its wheel directly, providing seamless power delivery and acceleration.

Top Speed:

The Nevera can reach a top speed of 258 mph (412 km/h), making it one of the fastest electric cars in the world.

Acceleration:

The car can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph (0 to 100 km/h) in just 1.85 seconds, 0 to 186 mph (0 to 300 km/h) in 9.3 seconds, and it completes a quarter mile in 8.6 seconds, setting new standards for electric hypercar acceleration.

3. Battery and Electric Range

Battery Pack:

The Nevera is powered by a 120 kWh lithium-manganese-nickel battery pack, designed in-house by Rimac. It is shaped to fit within the monocoque and provide a low center of gravity, improving handling and stability.

Range:

The Nevera has an estimated range of 340 miles (550 km) on a single charge according to WLTP standards, which is quite impressive given its performance capabilities.

Charging:

The Nevera supports DC fast charging up to 500 kW, which allows the battery to be recharged from 0 to 80% in about 19 minutes, making it one of the fastest-charging electric cars available.

4. Chassis and Handling

Monocoque Chassis:

The Nevera’s monocoque is a full carbon-fiber structure, enhancing both rigidity and crash protection. This structure includes an integrated battery pack and incorporates advanced crash zones for enhanced safety.

Suspension:

The Nevera features a double-wishbone suspension setup with electronically adjustable dampers, which can be tuned to optimize performance for different driving conditions and styles.

All-Wheel Drive:

With an electric motor on each wheel, the Nevera offers a fully variable all-wheel-drive system, allowing it to distribute power individually to each wheel, enhancing traction, stability, and cornering capabilities.

Brakes:

The car comes equipped with carbon-ceramic brakes with 6-piston calipers, offering exceptional stopping power to match its incredible speed. It also has regenerative braking, which can recharge the battery while braking and provide an energy-efficient way to slow down.

5. Interior and Technology

Interior Design:

The interior of the Nevera is a blend of luxury and high-tech functionality, featuring premium materials like Alcantara, carbon fiber, and leather. It is designed with a driver-centric layout, creating an immersive driving experience.

Infotainment:

The Nevera has a high-resolution, touch-screen infotainment system that displays vehicle performance data, navigation, and multimedia functions. Rimac offers over-the-air updates, allowing the car to receive new features and improvements remotely.

Digital Displays:

The car features three digital displays, including one for the driver (instrument cluster), a central infotainment screen, and a third screen in front of the passenger for monitoring performance data and other information.

AI and Driving Modes:

The Nevera is equipped with Rimac’s AI-powered “Driver Coach” mode, which provides real-time feedback on driving dynamics and assists the driver in optimizing their performance. Other driving modes include Range, Comfort, Sport, Track, and Drift modes, each optimizing different aspects of performance and handling.

6. Advanced Technology and Safety Features

Torque Vectoring:

The Nevera’s all-wheel torque vectoring system, called R-AWTV (Rimac All-Wheel Torque Vectoring), allows for precise control of power to each wheel independently, enhancing cornering capabilities and stability.

Driver Coach:

Using AI and a suite of sensors, the Driver Coach mode provides guidance on how to maximize performance, helping the driver achieve optimal lap times on the track.

Safety Features:

The Nevera is equipped with numerous active safety features, including adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, automatic emergency braking, and multiple airbags.

Autonomous Capabilities:

While not fully autonomous, the Nevera has advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS), including multiple cameras, radar, and ultrasonic sensors to assist with semi-autonomous driving.

7. Production and Exclusivity

Limited Production:

Rimac plans to produce only 150 units of the Nevera, making it a highly exclusive hypercar designed for collectors and enthusiasts alike.

Price:

The starting price of the Rimac Nevera is approximately $2.4 million USD, with customization options that can increase the price based on the owner’s preferences.

8. Customization Options

Bespoke Options:

Rimac offers extensive customization, allowing customers to choose from a variety of colors, materials, and finishes for both the interior and exterior. Rimac’s design team works with customers to create a personalized vehicle that reflects their tastes.

Special Editions:

Each Nevera can be uniquely tailored, and Rimac offers limited-edition options with exclusive design elements and materials for collectors who want an even more distinctive car.

9. Unique Aspects and Innovations

Electric Powertrain Innovation:

Rimac’s in-house electric motors, battery system, and powertrain components demonstrate the company’s engineering prowess and position it as a leader in electric hypercar technology.

Torque Vectoring and AI Integration:

The R-AWTV system and Driver Coach AI make the Nevera one of the most technologically advanced hypercars in terms of handling and driver assistance.

Record-Breaking Speed and Acceleration:

The Nevera’s acceleration times, quarter-mile time, and top speed set new benchmarks for production electric vehicles, demonstrating the potential of electric powertrains for high performance.

10. Competitors

The Nevera competes with other high-performance electric hypercars, including the Pininfarina Battista (which uses Rimac’s powertrain), Lotus Evija, and Tesla Roadster (second generation). These cars aim to showcase the future of high-speed electric performance and push the boundaries of what electric vehicles can achieve.

11. Legacy and Impact

Benchmark for Electric Hypercars:

The Rimac Nevera sets a new standard for electric hypercars, combining extreme performance with state-of-the-art technology and luxury.

Partnerships with Other Automakers:

Rimac’s technology has led to partnerships with major brands like Porsche, Bugatti, and Aston Martin, underscoring its influence in the high-performance EV sector.

A Vision for the Future of EVs:

The Nevera demonstrates Rimac’s commitment to innovation in electric mobility and shows what’s possible in the realm of electric performance cars.
