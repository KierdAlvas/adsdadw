Hennessey Venom F5

The Hennessey Venom F5 is a hypercar developed by Hennessey Special Vehicles, an American high-performance vehicle manufacturer. Launched with the intent of breaking speed records, the Venom F5 combines ultra-lightweight design with extreme power. Here’s an in-depth look at the car’s specifications and features:

1. Engine and Performance

Engine:

The Venom F5 is powered by a custom-built, twin-turbocharged 6.6-liter V8 engine, nicknamed “Fury.”

Horsepower:

The engine delivers a massive 1,817 horsepower and 1,193 lb-ft of torque, making it one of the most powerful production cars in the world.

Top Speed:

Hennessey designed the Venom F5 with the goal of surpassing 300 mph (482 km/h), and they have claimed a theoretical top speed of 311 mph (500 km/h).

Acceleration:

The car can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph (0 to 97 km/h) in under 3 seconds and 0 to 186 mph (0 to 300 km/h) in less than 10 seconds.

Transmission:

It is equipped with a 7-speed automated single-clutch transmission, designed for high-speed shifts and lightweight performance.

2. Chassis and Body

Chassis:

The Venom F5’s carbon-fiber monocoque chassis weighs just 86 kg (190 pounds), which keeps the overall weight of the vehicle incredibly low.

Body Material:

The entire body is also made from lightweight carbon fiber to enhance aerodynamics and reduce drag.

Aerodynamics:

The car has been engineered for maximum aerodynamic efficiency, with a drag coefficient of 0.39 Cd. The body’s design minimizes drag while maintaining downforce at high speeds.

Suspension:

It features double-wishbone suspension at the front and rear, with adjustable dampers that allow for optimal handling and performance.

3. Interior

Design:

The interior is designed to keep weight low and is relatively minimalist compared to other hypercars, though it still features high-end materials like Alcantara and carbon fiber.

Technology:

It comes with a 7-inch infotainment system that supports Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, as well as a high-resolution digital display for the driver. However, the focus remains on a track-ready experience.

Seating:

Racing-style seats provide strong support during high-speed driving.

4. Weight and Dimensions

Weight:

The Venom F5 has a curb weight of around 1,360 kg (2,998 pounds), which is achieved through the extensive use of carbon fiber and lightweight materials.

Dimensions:

It has a low and wide profile, designed to improve handling and stability at high speeds.

5. Production and Price

Limited Production:

Hennessey plans to build only 24 units of the Venom F5, making it extremely exclusive.

Price:

The base price for the Venom F5 is around $2.1 million USD, though customizations and additional features can increase this significantly.

6. Performance Goals

The Venom F5 was developed with the goal of becoming the world’s fastest production car, aiming to surpass existing records for top speed. While there is no official confirmation that it has hit 311 mph, Hennessey claims it has the capability.

7. Key Competitors

The Venom F5 competes with other hypercars aiming for record-breaking speeds, such as the Bugatti Chiron Super Sport 300+, Koenigsegg Jesko Absolut, and SSC Tuatara.

8. Unique Aspects

Exclusivity:

With only 24 units being produced, the Venom F5 is not only powerful but rare.

Design Philosophy:

car is designed to be both a performance icon and a statement piece, focusing on raw speed over luxury.

Hennessey’s Legacy:

It follows in the footsteps of the Hennessey Venom GT, a predecessor that also aimed to break speed records.
